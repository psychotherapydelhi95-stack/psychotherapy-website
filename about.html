About the Supervisor

A Partnership for Growth

Welcome. My name is [Supervisor Name], and I am a licensed psychotherapist and certified clinical supervisor. With over [Number] years of experience in [Modality/Specialty], I am dedicated to supporting the next generation of therapists.

My Philosophy: I believe supervision is a collaborative endeavor. It is not just about case management, but about fostering a deep understanding of the self-in-relation-to-other. We will work together examining transference, countertransference, and the subtle nuances of the therapeutic alliance.
