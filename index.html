Aim & Objectives

Upholding Principles of Practice

The group is meant to upheld the sacrosanct principles of Psychotherapy practice, research and training for the ultimate benefit of the client.

Our Objectives:
• To harness self-regulation in one’s own psychotherapy practice.
• To inculcate automaticity in introspection and reflection on one’s own work.
• To accept self, peer and supervisor supervision as indispensable part of one’s work.
