Supervision Formats

Tailored support to meet your professional needs.

Individual Supervision

One-on-one sessions focused on your specific caseload. We delve deep into case conceptualization, intervention strategies, and self-reflection.

  • 50-minute sessions
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Case report review

Group Supervision

Collaborative learning in a small group setting (max 6 participants). Benefit from diverse perspectives, peer support, and shared clinical wisdom.

  • 90-minute sessions
  • Monthly or Bi-weekly
  • Cost-effective

Online Consultations

Flexible remote supervision sessions via secure video conferencing. Accessibility for therapists in different locations or those needing flexible timings.

  • Secure platform
  • Same level of depth
  • Easy scheduling

Not sure which format fits you?

Contact me to discuss your current practice needs and we can decide together.
